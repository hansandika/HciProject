Gameplay

Kenshin Impact is an open world game where we can explore freely when we arrived at Teyvat - a world where the seven elements flow and converge. Teyvat is formed by seven elements: Anemo, Electro, Hydro, Pyro, Cryo, Dendro, and Geo. Your character can use that elements to fight or explore the wild world
