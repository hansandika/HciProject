About miHoYoMan:

"Game Tech Company Save The Earth"

miHoYoMan was founded in 2021 by three BINUS University graduate students who shared a passion for technology and ACG (anime, comics, and games) culture. MiHoYoMan has since gone on to become the original IP creator and interactive entertainment company that it is today.

In the future, miHoYoMan will continue to create more original products with a sincere dedication to transformation and technological innovation, continuing to pave its own path in the industry.

Company Milestones

2021 – Kenshin Impact is released worldwide on PlayStation4, PC, iOS, and Android with cross-platform co-op between mobile and PC.

Future – Just wait and see what fun surprises our otakus will bring the world next!
